Saving is easier with a little help

Simply Save offers you an easy way to build your savings. When you sign up for Simply Save, you decide:

  • How much to transfer per transaction (from $0.50 to $5)
  • What kind of transactions to include (debit purchase, ATM withdrawal or both)
  • How often savings will be transferred from your chequing account to the savings account (daily, weekly or biweekly)

And there are no set-up fees!

What to consider before starting your dispute

To dispute a transaction on your TD Access Card, make sure you address the following before submitting a dispute:

1. The transaction is related to a purchase you made with your TD Access Card:
To submit a dispute, it must be for a purchase made with your TD Access Card. If you find a transaction on your statement that's from a merchant you haven't transacted with, then the charge could be fraudulent. If you suspect the transaction is fraudulent, please contact EasyLine (TD Access Cards or ATM transactions: 1-866-222-3456) immediately.

2. You attempt to resolve with the merchant prior to initiating a dispute:
If you have a dispute with the merchant about a purchase you made, please first attempt to resolve the issue directly with the merchant. Once you speak to the merchant, your issue may be resolved, and your money refunded. However, if you are unable to resolve the issue directly with the merchant, refer to the next step.

3. You submit a transaction dispute request within the appropriate timeframe:
Proceed to submit a transaction dispute within 30 days of the date you have received notice of the transaction as per your account agreement.
